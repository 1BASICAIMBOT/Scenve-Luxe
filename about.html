Our Story

Born from a Love
of Fine Fragrance

From a small studio in Grasse, France, to homes around the world — Scenve Luxe was built on the belief that luxury should be personal, intentional, and truly beautiful.

Our Origins

The Art of Scent,
Reimagined

Scenve Luxe was founded in 2018 by Élise Laurent — a classically trained perfumer who spent a decade working with the finest raw ingredients in Grasse before launching her own house.

Frustrated with the perfume industry's tendency toward mass production and artificial ingredients, Élise set out to create something different: fragrances that are honest about what they're made of, and extraordinary in how they wear.

Every scent in the Scenve Luxe collection is made in small batches using ethically sourced natural ingredients — from Grasse jasmine to Bulgarian rose to Indonesian oud. We work with the same flower growers and oud distillers year after year, because quality doesn't happen by accident.

What We Believe

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by a simple belief: that real luxury starts with integrity.

Exceptional Quality

We use only the finest natural ingredients, sourced from trusted growers we've worked with for years. No shortcuts, no synthetics where naturals will do better.

Ethical Sourcing

From the rose farmers in Bulgaria to the oud distillers in Indonesia, we build relationships, not just supply chains. Fair wages and sustainable harvesting always.

Cruelty Free

We are proud to be 100% cruelty free. No animal testing, ever — on ingredients, finished products, or anywhere in our supply chain.

Small Batch Craft

Small batches mean more attention, better quality, and fresher fragrance. We don't make more than we can make well.
