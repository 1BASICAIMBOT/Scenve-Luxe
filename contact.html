Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. Express (1–2 days) is also available. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 days.

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns on unopened products. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us and we'll make it right — no questions asked.

Can I try a fragrance before buying?

Yes! We offer sample sets — 5 fragrances in 2ml vials for $25 (credited toward a full purchase). Add them to your cart under "Gift Sets."

Are your fragrances natural?

Our fragrances are predominantly natural — we use ethically sourced botanical ingredients wherever possible. A small number of safe, IFRA-approved aromatic molecules are used to enhance stability and longevity.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Every order ships in our signature Scenve Luxe gift box with tissue paper and a ribbon. Perfect as a gift, at no extra charge. Add a personalised message at checkout.
